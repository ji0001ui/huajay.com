Molding The Future

We strive to consistently offer the most efficient and sustainable high quality, custom plastic injection molding solutions while providing comprehensive value in a globally competitive marketplace.

Why choose Us

Since we began in 1997 as a custom injection molder, the foundation for all we do has centered on our commitment to excellence. We’ve proven this through our long-term relationships with our customers and suppliers, and it’s driven by the integrity we have to work as a partner with each.

Variety of Services

As far as we’re concerned, the range of our services includes the most demanded plastic manufacturing and design needs of America’s manufacturing community. With our eyes on a further advance in our capabilities, we look forward to expanding what we offer.

Quality Above All

Being fully certified for providing either of the plastic molding services we do, our company values the end-products quality above everything else. We have so many returning clients for a reason – with the reason being the highest standards of quality we uphold.
