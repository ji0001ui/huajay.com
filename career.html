Day/night Shift Casual Production Workers

We require Casual Production Staff to assist our Manufacturing and Assembly teams. You'll be working alongside our friendly staff to pick, sort, grade and assemble.

Requirements
  • 2+ years experiences in manufacturing.
  • Able to work shifts:7am-7pm (12 hour shift) or 9pm-5am (8 hour shift).
  • Good Communication Skills
  • Team Player
What we Expect from you?
  • Assembling and packing finished product ready for shipment.
  • Ensuring our products meet company and customer standards.

ISO Management Rep

Promote a safe work environment that drives continuous improvement in all aspects of employee health and safety.

Requirements
  • Minimum of 2 years college or equivalent knowledge.
  • ISO 9000/2000 experience.
  • Knowledge in current ISO 9001 standard
  • Six Sigma knowledge.
  • Background in team building and skills.
  • Strong motivational and communication skills.
  • High level of organization and managing skills.
What we Expect from you?
  • Maintain the Quality Management System using teams and Six Sigma to develop a consist operating system that meets corporate objectives.
  • Oversee Internal ISO Auditor group in conjunction with Internal Lead Auditor.
  • Oversee ISO Team Leaders and insure that they are completing their ISO responsibilities outlined in their job description.

Production Supervisor

Individual will be responsible for the supervision and direction of assigned employees and for all processes, safety, production, quality, and quantity standards in organizational unit.

Requirements
  • 4 year-degree or equivalent combination of experience and education.
  • 5+ years experience in a leadership position with proven ability to deliver desired results.
  • Demonstrated competences in the following areas: Employee Safety and Security, Problem Solving, Leadership, Change Management, Delegation, Managing People and Processes, Business Acumen, Cost Containment, and Organizational Support.
  • Must have strong interpersonal and leadership skills with ability to motivate employees.
  • Strong technical, business, and organizational skills.
  • Able to quickly learn production process and company procedures.
  • Ability to utilize a variety of computer software (MS Products, etc.).
  • Able to work in a production environment, recognizing various sounds and alarms.
What we Expect from you?
  • Responsible for achieving safety, quality, and productivity goals in assigned Production Department.
  • Manage production and support activities for shift employees.
  • Responsible for employee development, conduct and overall performance of Shift/Department.
  • Completes responsibilities in accordance with the organization's policies and procedures, and applicable laws.
  • Delegates work activities to others to efficiently manage their unit.
  • Motivates and coaches employees through good communication and frequent performance feedback. Identifies and coordinates additional training as needed.
  • Assesses employee readiness for promotion, interviews and identifies appropriate candidates for vacancies.
  • Drive cost saving initiatives, efficiency gains, and process improvements in the Shift/Dept.
  • Responsible for accurate production reporting and timekeeping.
