Expert Plastic Injection Mold Making Since 1997

Our clients have ideas. We, Huyjay Plastics, have the knowledge, tools, and expertise to make them a reality. Our custom plastic injection molding services are backed by our over 20 years of experience in designing and creating plastic parts and products. Many industries have some form of plastic components or parts, and many of them use plastic injection molding. We serve all of these industries. The process of building custom injection molds is one that Huajay knows well.

Good Design

Our years of experience working with diverse client means we are able to take even the most basic design idea from our customers and make it into a quality product.

Building the Mold

The process of plastic mold building is one that we have done many times. After we have received the final design for the product, we create a mold out of steel. No detail is left out as we machine the block into the shape of the part. Each piece is then put together in a mold base.

Variety of Industries

We are able to work with many different clients in a wide variety of industries, creating molds to their specifications. Some if these include the automotive, aerospace, industrial products, health care, electronics, and many more.
